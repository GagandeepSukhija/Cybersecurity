Three core principles of protecting data and systems.

Confidentiality:
- Password Protection
- Data Encryption
- Access Controls (Role-based)
- 2FA

Integrity:
- Checksums and hash functions
- Digital Signatures
- Version Control Systems
- Audit Logs

Availability:
- Redundancy (backups, RAID)
- Load Balancing
- DDoS Protection
- High Availability architecture
